Pizza

Description

Very good and makes for great leftovers too.

Bakery style pizza is suprisingly light and crisp.

Ingredients

  • 1 (16oz) pizza dough
  • 1 (28oz) can plain crushed tomatoes
  • 8oz low-moisture whole-mile mozzarella, thin sliced
  • 1/4 cup grated Pecorino Romano cheese

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 450 degrees F.
  2. On a floured surface, use your hands to shape the dough into a flat disc. Transfer the dough to a lighly greased pizza pan.
  3. Spread the sauce onto the top of the dough, leaving space around the edges for crust.
  4. Sprinkle the mozzarella cheese on top.
  5. Bake for 13 minutes. The cheese should be browning and bubbly.
  6. Sprinkle the Romano cheese on top as soon as the pizza comes out of the oven
